A browser-based version of the card game 6 Second Scribbles. You get a category card showing 10 things to draw — 6 seconds per item. Other players guess what you drew. Points go to correct guessers and the artist.

Built in about 60 minutes with GitHub Copilot while visiting family in Sweden, when the physical card deck wasn't available. Three difficulty levels: easy, medium, and hard.
