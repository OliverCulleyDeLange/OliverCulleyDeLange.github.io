Location Alarm is a Kotlin Multiplatform app that fires an alarm when you reach a destination — useful for napping on trains, buses, or long drives without missing your stop.

Set a pin on a map, start the alarm, then put your phone away. When you get close enough, it wakes you up. No need to keep the screen on.

Built with **Kotlin Multiplatform** targeting Android and iOS, using Compose Multiplatform for the UI.
