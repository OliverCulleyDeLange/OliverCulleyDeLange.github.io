Most mortgage calculators answer a single question — "what's my monthly payment?" — and stop there. The more useful question is usually *how does that number change if I stretch or shorten the term?*

This one plots the whole curve. Enter a loan amount and an interest rate; the chart shows the monthly payment for every term length from 5 to 40 years, with a slider to highlight a specific term and see the total interest you'd pay over its lifetime.

Built as a single Astro page with vanilla JS and a hand-drawn SVG chart — no framework, no chart library, no network calls.
